About Lotto Soudal

Lotto–Soudal (UCI team code: LTS) is a Belgian professional cycling team sponsored by the Belgian lottery and Soudal, an adhesives manufacturer. For the 2016 and 2017 editions of Paris-Nice, and the 2018 Giro d'Italia, the team raced under the name Lotto-FixAll, and in a different colour of kit, to promote a particular product from the Soudal range.. The same organisations also sponsor a women's cycling team, Lotto–Soudal Ladies.
